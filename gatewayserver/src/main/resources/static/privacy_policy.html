Privacy Policy

How we collect and protect you data

Last Updated: October, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use Strangerblink, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, age, gender, country
  • Chat Content: Messages you send and receive (stored temporarily for service operation all encrypted)
  • Technical Data: IP address
  • Connection Data: Online status, connection timestamps
Automatically Collected Information
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our chat services
  • Connect you with random users
  • Ensure platform safety and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share data in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Safety Emergencies: To prevent harm or illegal activities

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • Chat Messages: Temporary storage for service operation
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized for improvement
  • Legal Requirements: As required by applicable laws

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve our services

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Monitoring for suspicious activities

8. Children's Privacy

Strangerblink is not intended for users under the age of 18. We don't encourage children to visit stranger chat sites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through our platform. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@strangerblink.fun
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
