About strangerblink

Connecting people across the globe through safe and meaningful conversations.

Meet the Developer

Sandesh Adhikari

Electronics and Communication Engineering Student

IOE WRC Pokhara

strangerblink was created by Sandesh Adhikari, an Electronics and Communication Engineering student at IOE WRC Pokhara. As a passionate developer and innovator, Sandesh combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of user needs to create meaningful connections in the digital space.

Our Mission

"To create a safe, engaging, and inclusive platform where people from all walks of life can connect, communicate, and build meaningful relationships through random chat experiences."

What Makes strangerblink Unique

Safety First

Community Impact

Since our launch, strangerblink has facilitated millions of conversations and helped users:

  • Overcome social anxiety through safe practice
  • Learn about different cultures and perspectives
  • Practice new languages with native speakers
  • Find support and friendship during challenging times
  • Expand their social networks globally
