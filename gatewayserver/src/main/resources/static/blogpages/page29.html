Accessibility Features

Dark Mode: More Than Just Aesthetic

Our dark mode isn't just a color inversion - it's a carefully designed experience with multiple benefits:

Reduced Eye Strain - Especially in low-light conditions
Battery Savings - Up to 30% on OLED screens
Better Sleep - Reduced blue light exposure in evenings
Reduced Glare - Improved readability in bright environments

Dark Mode Features

1. Multiple Dark Themes

Choose from several carefully calibrated dark themes:

True Dark: Pure black background for maximum battery savings on OLED displays
Dark Gray: Softer dark gray for reduced eye strain while maintaining depth
Blue Light Filter: Warmer dark theme with reduced blue light emission

2. Smart Auto-Switching

Dark mode can automatically activate based on:

Sunset/Sunrise: Follows your local daylight hours
System Settings: Syncs with your device's dark mode preference
Custom Schedule: Set your own preferred hours for dark/light mode

Accessibility Features Overview

Visual Accessibility

Font Size & Scaling

Adjust text size from 12px to 24px with smooth scaling that maintains interface integrity.

High Contrast Mode

Enhanced color contrast ratios for users with low vision or color blindness.

Reduced Motion

Minimize or eliminate animations for users with vestibular disorders or motion sensitivity.

Motor & Navigation Accessibility

Keyboard Navigation

Full keyboard accessibility with logical tab order and keyboard shortcuts.

Large Click Targets

All interactive elements have minimum 44px touch targets for easier selection.

Voice Control Support

Compatible with popular voice control systems for hands-free operation.

Hearing & Audio Accessibility

Voice Message Transcription

Automatic transcription of voice messages for deaf and hard-of-hearing users.

Visual Sound Indicators

Visual cues for incoming voice messages and call notifications.

How to Enable Accessibility Features

Step 1: Access Settings

Tap your profile picture → Settings → Accessibility

Step 2: Customize Features

Toggle individual features on/off and adjust settings to your preference

Step 3: Save Preferences

Your settings sync across all devices automatically

Step 4: Test & Refine

Try different combinations and adjust as needed for optimal comfort

Accessibility Tips for Better Chatting

For Better Readability

Use the "Dyslexia-Friendly" font option if you find certain fonts easier to read. Combine with increased line spacing for even better readability.

Color Blindness Support

Enable color blindness filters that adjust our interface colors to be distinguishable for users with various types of color vision deficiency.

Motor Impairment Assistance

Use voice commands for common actions or enable "Sticky Keys" for easier keyboard navigation if you have difficulty with simultaneous key presses.

Advanced Features

Screen Reader Optimization

Our platform is fully compatible with popular screen readers including:

  • JAWS (Windows)
  • NVDA (Windows)
  • VoiceOver (macOS/iOS)
  • TalkBack (Android)

All interface elements include proper ARIA labels and semantic HTML for accurate screen reader interpretation.

Customizable Notification Systems

Tailor notifications to your specific needs:

Visual Alerts: Customize flash patterns, colors, and durations
Haptic Feedback: Adjust vibration patterns for different notification types
Audio Cues: Choose from various sound profiles or upload custom sounds

Continuous Improvement

Our accessibility features are constantly evolving based on:

  • User feedback from our accessibility testing community
  • WCAG 2.1 AA compliance standards and beyond
  • Emerging technologies in assistive tech
  • Partnerships with disability advocacy organizations
  • Regular audits by third-party accessibility experts
We Want Your Feedback

Help us improve! If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for new features, please contact our accessibility team at accessibility@strangerchat.com. Your input directly shapes our development roadmap.
