Getting Started with Custom Stickers

1. Access the Sticker Creator

Find the sticker creation tool in your chat interface. Click the sticker icon, then select "Create New Sticker" to begin.

Location: Chat window → Sticker button (smiley face) → Create New Sticker

2. Choose Your Creation Method

We offer multiple ways to create stickers:

Upload Images: Turn your photos or drawings into stickers
Use Templates: Start with our pre-designed templates and customize them
Draw from Scratch: Use our drawing tools to create original artwork

3. Customize and Enhance

Add text, effects, and animations to make your stickers unique:

Text Options: Choose from various fonts, colors, and sizes
Effects Library: Add shadows, glows, or special filters

Advanced Sticker Creation Tips

Color Psychology

Use colors strategically - warm colors for excitement and energy, cool colors for calm and thoughtful expressions.

Readability Matters

Keep text minimal and ensure it's readable even at small sizes. Simple, bold fonts work best.

Optimal Sizing

Create stickers that look good at both small preview sizes and when expanded in chat.

Animation Best Practices

Keep animations short (2-3 seconds) and loop smoothly. Avoid overly complex movements.

Technical Specifications

Supported Formats and Limits

  • Image Formats: PNG, JPG, GIF (transparent backgrounds supported)
  • Maximum File Size: 5MB per sticker
  • Dimensions: Recommended 512x512 pixels (scales automatically)
  • Animation Length: Up to 5 seconds for animated stickers
  • Storage Limit: 100 custom stickers per user
  • Sharing Limits: Up to 50 stickers can be shared in one pack
