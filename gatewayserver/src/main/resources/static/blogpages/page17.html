Privacy Settings: Take Control of Your Data

A detailed walkthrough of our privacy features and how to use them.

Privacy Officer
February 27, 2024

StrangerChat prioritizes your privacy and provides a variety of tools to control your personal data. Understanding and managing your privacy settings ensures safer and more comfortable interactions on our platform.

1. Account Visibility Settings

You can decide who can see your online status, profile, and activity. Adjust settings to stay anonymous or visible only to certain users.

2. Chat Privacy Options

Enable or disable features such as screenshot notifications, message expiration, and filtering messages from unknown users.

3. Data Sharing Controls

Manage what data is shared with third-party integrations or advertisers. You can opt-out of non-essential data sharing to keep your interactions private.

4. Blocking and Reporting

StrangerChat allows you to block or report users who make you uncomfortable. This ensures a safer community and personalized moderation.

5. Two-Factor Authentication

Enhance account security with two-factor authentication. Linking your phone or authentication app adds an extra layer of protection against unauthorized access.

Tips for Maintaining Privacy

Regularly Review Settings

Check your privacy settings periodically to ensure they reflect your current preferences.

Limit Sharing Personal Info

Avoid sharing sensitive personal details like home address, financial info, or private identifiers in chats.

Be Mindful of Third-Party Apps

Only connect trusted apps and integrations. Review permissions before granting access to your data.
