Meet the Developer

BlinkRandom was created by Sandesh Adhikari, an Electronics and Communication Engineering student at IOE WRC Pokhara. As a passionate developer and innovator, Sandesh combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of user needs to create meaningful connections in the digital space. This platform represents his commitment to building technology that brings people together in safe and engaging ways.

Our Mission
